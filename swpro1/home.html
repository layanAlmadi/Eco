The rewards system on our recycling website is a mechanism designed to motivate and encourage users to actively participate in recycling processes. Through this system, users earn reward points for every contribution they make, such as providing recyclable materials. These points serve as a step towards enhancing environmental awareness and motivating individuals to continuously participate in achieving recycling goals and preserving the environment for future generations.
